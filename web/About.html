A Little About Me

My name is Andrew Wilkinson and I am a recent graduate from the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) with a MS in Game Design and Development. I received my BS in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Lowell, but felt that to get closer my dreams of game development I should make a new academic pursuit. RIT was a wonderful experience full of wonderful people who gave me the resources and environment to teach myself the skills required for game development.

I enjoy playing a large variety of games and genres, but most of my game time is spent on the digital distribution platform, Steam. My account is at Mr. Android, feel free to friend me! I'm also a founding member of a small gaming community known as The FUN-Gineers. Check out our website, The FUN-Gineers, it was created by the same person who made the background and color choice of this website, Jason Patrick.

This website is here to show projects that I have worked on over the years and present my thought process when it comes to new tasks. Thank you for reading!

~Andrew Wilkinson (Mr. Android)
